Evidence Based UX/UI Designer

Hi! My name is Jonathan Kaplan and I am an empathy and research driven UX/UI designer with a focus in user research and usability testing currently living in Atlanta, Georgia. I am currently enrolled in the Georgia Tech UX/UI Bootcamp and serve tables on the side. I am actively looking for UX Research and UX Design positions, and would ideally use my newly established skills within the gaming/entertainment industry.
